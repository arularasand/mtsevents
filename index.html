MTS Tamil Schools Annual Day

Annual Day 2026

Join us for a full day celebrating our students, teachers, volunteers, and families. The program includes stage performances, trophy distribution, certificate distribution, and lunch stalls.

Date
To be announced
Time
10:00 AM - 3:30 PM
Location
MTS Tamil Schools

Event Highlights

Trophy Distribution

Recognizing outstanding student achievements, competition winners, and special participation.

Lunch Stalls

Enjoy lunch, snacks, and refreshments from community food stalls during the afternoon break.

Certificate Distribution

Certificates will be presented to students for completion, participation, and Tamil learning milestones.

Annual Day Schedule

10:00 AM

Welcome and Opening Prayer

Guest arrival, seating, Tamil Thai Vazhthu, and opening remarks.

10:30 AM

Student Cultural Programs

Tamil speeches, songs, dances, skits, and classroom presentations.

12:00 PM

Trophy Distribution

Awards for competitions, excellence, and special accomplishments.

12:45 PM

Lunch Stalls Open

Lunch and refreshments available for families and volunteers.

2:00 PM

Certificate Distribution

Certificates for students by grade level and program participation.

3:00 PM

Closing Remarks

Vote of thanks, volunteer appreciation, and final announcements.
